## Polar graphs

![Basic polar graphs](Static/basic-polar-graphs.png)

Curves of the form
$$\Large
\begin{flalign}
r = a(p + q \ cos \ \theta)&&
\end{flalign}$$
where $p, q > 0$, will:
- be circular when $p = 0$
- be a cardioid when $p = q$
- have a 'dimple' when $1 \le \frac{p}{q} \lt 2$
- be 'egg-shaped' when $\frac{p}{q} \ge 2$
