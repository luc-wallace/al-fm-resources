### Inverting a 3x3 matrix

1. Get minors
2. Transpose
3. Multiply by $\frac{1}{det M}$

### Cross product

$$
\Large
\begin{flalign}
\begin{pmatrix}
a_0\\
a_1\\
a_2
\end{pmatrix}
\times
\begin{pmatrix}
b_0\\
b_1\\
b_2
\end{pmatrix}
= det
\begin{bmatrix}
i && j &&k \\
a_0 && a_1 && a_2\\
b_0 && b_1 && b_2
\end{bmatrix}&&
\end{flalign}
$$
