# Further Maths Formulae

Below is a list of formulae that is not included in the formula book. 

#### Simpson's rule

$$
\Large
\begin{flalign}
\int_{a}^{b}y \; dx \ = \frac{h}{3} [ y_0 + y_n + 4(y_1+y_3+ \dots + y_{n-1}) + 2(y_2+y_4+\dots+y_{n-2})]&&
\end{flalign}
$$
where  $$
\Large
\begin{flalign}
h = \frac{b - a}{2}&&
\end{flalign}
$$
